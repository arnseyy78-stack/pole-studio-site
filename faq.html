No experience is required. You can start from your current level and build your strength, confidence, and technique over time.

Message us directly on WhatsApp or use the Book page to send your booking request.

1 Class is ₱650 plus a ₱150 booking fee.

Wear comfortable activewear that allows easy movement and support during class.
